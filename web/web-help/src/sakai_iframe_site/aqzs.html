Changing the size of your site's Site Information Display box

Note: To complete the procedures in this document, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and roles: Overview.

To change the size of your site's Site Information Display box:

  1. Select a course site or project site.

  2. If your site's home page is not already open, from the menubar, click Home.

  3. In the Site Information Display box, click Options.

    Note: Because the title of this box is customizable, it may be titled something other than "Site Information Display" on your site; however, it will always be located on your site's home page just beside the menubar.

  4. To change the height of the box, from the "Frame Height" drop-down menu, select a height. You cannot change the width of the box.

    Note: Most web pages display better in a taller box (600 or more pixels).

  5. To save your changes, click Update Options.
