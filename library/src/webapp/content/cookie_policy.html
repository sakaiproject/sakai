Use of Cookies

  • jsMath – wiki, has the user been shown the missing jsMath fonts warning message?
  • JSESSIONID – session status (secure)
  • profile2-tab – stores the current tab index in the Profile tool

The only cookie that links to personal data is JSESSIONID.

We consider all of these cookies to be essential for the service, none of them persist after the session has ended.

In addition to the above site owner can develop content that sets cookies. We cannot effectively police these third party cookies and hope that they are only used when necessary.
