Sign-up Tool: Creating meetings

There are three types of events or meetings:

  • Multiple slots: a single time slot can be divided into any number of meetings of equal length. For example, a two hour meeting could have four half-hour slots or eight quarter-hour slots. For each slot there is a maximum number of participants. Participants are required to sign up.

  • Single slot: a single time slot is created and the number of participants can be limited or unlimited. Participants are required to sign up.

  • Announcement: creates a single time slot for an event or meeting. Participants are not required to sign up.

To create a meeting:

  1. Click Add, then choose the type of meeting and complete all the required information about dates, times and place of meeting.

  2. Check the appropriate boxes to manage email notifications and the display of participant names.

  3. Publish by clicking either Publish or Assign Participants & Publish. You have the option of assigning participants or allowing them to choose their own time slots.

Note: To create similar meetings without re-typing all the information, consider copying a meeting and changing details as needed. To copy a meeting, click on the meeting, then click Copy at the top of the screen.
