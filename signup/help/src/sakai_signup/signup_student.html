Sign-up tool: Student view

If your instructor is using the Sign-up tool you will see it listed on the left side of the page.

  1. Click Sign-up and you will see a list of meetings set up by the instructor.

  2. Follow the directions to sign up for a meeting. If the meeting is full you may add your name to the Wait List and you will be notified by email if space becomes available and you are added to the list of attendees.

    Note: There is a comment field where you can type a message which is seen only by the instructor.

  3. You may cancel any appointment you have signed up for by clicking the Meeting Title, then Cancel Sign-up.

  4. At the instructor's discretion, you may be able to see the names of participants who have signed up.
