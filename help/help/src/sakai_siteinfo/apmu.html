To grant global access to a course or project site, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the course or project site, click Site Info.

  2. Click Edit Access... , and then click Global Access... .

  3. Set access controls for your site as follows:

    • If you wish to give access to your site to the entire community, select Site can be joined by anyone with authorization to login. When the "Role Selection" options appear, choose a role for all the users who may join your site.
    • If you want your site open only to users you add, select Open only to those you specifically add.

  4. Click Continue, and then click Finish.
