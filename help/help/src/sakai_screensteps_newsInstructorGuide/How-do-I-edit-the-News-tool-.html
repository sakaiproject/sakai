How do I edit the News tool?

Go to News.

Select the News item from the Tool Menu of your site.

(If a News item is not already active in the site, you will need to to Site Info > Edit Tools to add it to your site first.)

Click Options.

Enter the News item information and save.

  1. Enter a tool title.
  2. Enter a page title for the tool. (The page title is the title that will display in the Tool Menu of your site.)
  3. Enter the URL of your RSS feed.
  4. Click Update Options to save your settings.
