What is My Calendar in the Account Menu?

Use My Calendar in the Account Menu to view deadlines, assessments, and other important events in calendar format.

My Calendar in the Account Menu shares information with Calendar in the Home Dashboard.

Viewing My Calendar

Open the Account Menu.

Click your Account icon to open the Account Menu.

Open My Calendar.

Click My Calendar to unfurl the calendar and view upcoming events.

Jump to an event in My Calendar.

Click a date with an event in My Calendar to jump to the site that's offering the event.
