How do I view and navigate to all of my sites?

View all your sites in a single list to quickly jump to a specific site, pin sites, or take stock of the sites in which you participate.

There are several ways to view and navigate to all your sites:

  • View All SitesÂ 
  • Worksite Setup

Navigating with View All Sites

Select the View All Sites icon in the top navigation.

In the header area of the page, select the grid icon (View All Sites).

You may also get to the View All Sites list from the link in the bottom left of the tool menu.

Select a site title to open the site.

Navigating with Worksite Setup

For complete information, see What is Worksite Setup?.

In your Home site, select Worksite Setup.

Click a site title to open the site.

Note: Inactive or Hidden sites display in Membership or Worksite Setup only.
