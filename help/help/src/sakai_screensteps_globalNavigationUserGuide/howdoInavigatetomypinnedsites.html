How do I navigate to my pinned sites?

In the Pinned sites area, open sites or open specific tools within sites that you've pinned.

Select the title of a site in the Pinned sites area to jump to that site.

Expand a site to jump to a specific tool

  1. Click the chevron icon associated with the site to expand the list of active tools in the site.
  2. Click a tool in the site to jump to the tool.
