What is Dark Mode?

Dark Mode or Dark Theme allows you to change the overall system color scheme to light text on a dark background, as opposed to the default dark text on light background. Utilizing Dark Mode offers benefits such as reducing your device's energy consumption and minimizing eye strain when working in low-light environments.

If your device's operating system is set to dark mode, the system will automatically enable Dark Mode in the application.

Note: If you don't see one or both of the methods below, it's possible that Dark Mode has not been enabled in your system.

Enabling Dark Mode via the Account menu

Click your Account icon.

In the site header area of the page, click your Account icon.

Select the Dark Mode option.

Verify the update to Dark Mode.

Enabling Dark Theme via Preferences

In your Home site, select the Preferences tool.

Select Theme.

Select Dark Theme and click Update Preferences.

Verify the update to Dark Theme.

Your screen will change to display light text on a dark background.
