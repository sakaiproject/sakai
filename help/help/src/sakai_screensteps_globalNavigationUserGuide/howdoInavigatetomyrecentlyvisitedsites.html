How do I navigate to my recently-visited sites?

In the Recent sites area, you will see a list of the sites that you've visited recently.

Sites that you've pinned will not appear within the Most recent sites area.

Select the title of a site in Recent sites area to jump to that site.

Expand the list of tools in a site within Recent sites to jump to the selected tool.

  1. Click the chevron icon associated with the site to expand the list of active tools in the site.
  2. Click a tool in the site to jump to the tool.
