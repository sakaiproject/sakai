What are the Quick Links in the site header?

Use Quick Links to access popular web links to locations outside of the system.

Quick Links has been configured by your system's administrators and may appear differently than the examples in this article.

Note: If you do not see the Quick Links icons, that means you institution has not enabled this feature.

Open Quick Links.

While using any tool in the system, click the link icon (Quick Links) in the site header area of the page.

Select a link.

Select a link from the Quick Links area. The link will open the new location in a separate tab or window in your browser.
