What are the Home Message Center Notifications?

The Message Center in Home displays the count of Messages and Forum posts for all of the sites in which you are enrolled. New messages are indicated by a number and envelope icon. If there are no new messages or posts, the word "none" will be displayed.

  • Clicking on the title of the site will take you to the homepage for that site.
  • Clicking on the New Messages indicator will take you directly to the Message Inbox for that site.
  • Clicking on the New in Forums indicator will take you directly to the Forums for that site.
