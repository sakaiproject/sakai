How do I reorder items on a Lessons page?

Instructors can reorder the placement of items on a Lessons Page.

Go to Lessons.

Click on the Lessons Page Title (e.g. Unit #1) in the Tool Menu to display the page.

Note: The default or blank Lessons page contains text and images that explain the basic functionality of the Lessons tool.

Click Reorder.

Zoom

Drag and drop the items into the desired order.

Zoom

Click Save.

Zoom

After saving, you will return to the Lessons page with the items displayed in the new order.
