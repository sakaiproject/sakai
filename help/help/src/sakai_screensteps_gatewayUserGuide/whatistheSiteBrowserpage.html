What is the Site Browser page?

The Site Browser page lets you search the list of sites in the system. You may search for sites by type (course or project), as well as by term for courses or by title for projects.

Search results list

Once you have entered your search parameters, any sites that are set to be visible in the Site Browser will appear in the search results list.

Selecting the link for a site will take you to the site. For sites requiring authentication to access, you will be prompted to log in to the system first.
