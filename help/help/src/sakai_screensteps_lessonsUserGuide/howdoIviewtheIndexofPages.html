How do I view the Index of Pages?

The hierarchy of all Lessons pages in a site is displayed under the Index of Pages button in the top right corner of the Lessons tool.

Go to Lessons.

Click on the Lessons page title in the Tool Menu to display the page.

Note: The default or blank Lessons page contains text and images that explain the basic functionality of the Lessons tool.

Click Index of pages.

Click Show Items to view all items on each page.

Click Tool Home button or on any of the page links to return to Lessons view.

Click on the Lessons page title in the breadcrumbs or on any of the page links to return to Lessons view.
