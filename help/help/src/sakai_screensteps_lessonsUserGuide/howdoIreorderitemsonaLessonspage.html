How do I reorder items on a Lessons page?

Instructors can reorder the placement of items on a Lessons page.

Go to Lessons.

If you titled your page something other than Lessons, select the title of the page as it appears in your Tool Menu.

Click Reorder.

Drag and drop the items into the desired order.

Click Save.

After saving, you will return to the Lessons page with the items displayed in the new order.
