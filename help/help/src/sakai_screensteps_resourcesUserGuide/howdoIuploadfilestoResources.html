How do I upload files to Resources?

Go to Resources.

Select the Resources tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click Actions, and then select Upload Files.

  1. Click the Actions option associated with a folder.
  2. Select Upload Files.

Drag and drop files from your computer.

Drag files from your computer and drop them in the Drop files to upload, or click here to browse box.

Or browse your computer for files.

Click the Drop files to upload, or click here to browse button. This action will open your computer's file upload window where you can browse for and select the files that you want to upload.

Note the file awaiting upload.

Thumbnail images of files awaiting upload confirmation will display in the Drop files to upload, or click here to browse box.

Click Continue.

Click the Continue button that's near the bottom of the page to confirm the file upload.
