How do I remove a file or folder in Resources?

Go to Resources.

Select the Resources tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Select one or more items, and then click Move to Trash.

  1. Check the boxes associated with the files or folders that you wish to remove.
  2. Click Move to Trash.

Note: if you want to remove one item at a time, you can use Actions > Move to Trash instead.

Click Remove once again to confirm.
