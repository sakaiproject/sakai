How do I view the content of an Bullhorn alert?

If you have any Bullhorn alerts, you can view them by clicking on the item to view more information. Bullhorns are grouped by tool and date.

Click on the Bullhorn icon in the top navigation to view a list of all current alerts.

Click on a tool and date heading in the list to view all alerts of that type.

Clicking on an individual alert item will take you to directly to that item within the site.

The selected item will display.
