How do I change the calendar view in the Schedule tool?

In Schedule, you can view your calendar by day, week, month, or year. You can also set the default view for your calendar.

Go to Schedule.

Select the Schedule tool from the Tool Menu in your site. (Remember that it may also be called Calendar.)

Select the desired view in the drop-down menu.
