How do I view the course grade override log in Gradebook?

Go to Gradebook.

Select Gradebook from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click the arrow icon and then select Course Grade Override Log.

  1. Click the arrow icon (Open menu) associated with a student in the Course Grade column.
  2. Select Course Grade Override Log.

View the log.

The Course Grade Override Log displays the date and time when the Course Grade was overridden, as well as the change in grade and the username of the user that made the change.
