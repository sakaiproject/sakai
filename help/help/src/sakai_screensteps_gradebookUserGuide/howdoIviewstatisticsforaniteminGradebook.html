How do I view statistics for an item in Gradebook?

Go to Gradebook.

Select Gradebook from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click the arrow icon and then select View Grade Statistics.

  1. Click the arrow icon (Open menu) associated with an item.
  2. Click View Grade Statistics.

View the statistics.

  1. Based on the current available scores for the Gradebook item you've selected, Grade Statistics lists:
    • Average (mean) grade
    • Median grade
    • Standard deviation
    • Lowest grade
    • Highest grade
    • Total graded
  2. Click Done to leave Grade Statistics.
