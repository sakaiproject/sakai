How do I display the course grade to students in Gradebook?

Calculated course grades are hidden from students by default. Use Settings > Grade Release Rules to display the calculated course grades.

Go to Gradebook.

Select Gradebook from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click Settings.

Configure the display of the final course grade.

  1. Open Grade Release Rules.
  2. Check Display final course grade to students.
  3. Check Letter Grade, Percentage, and/or Points to display these values to students as part of their course grade.
  4. Click Save Changes.
