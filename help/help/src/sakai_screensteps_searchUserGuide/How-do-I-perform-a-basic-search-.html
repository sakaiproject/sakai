How do I perform a basic search?

Go to Search.

Select the Search tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Enter your search term/s.

Zoom

On the Search page, you will find an entry box.

  1. Enter the words that you want to search for. For example, "ocean".
  2. Click the Search button.

View search results.

Zoom
  1. Your search results will be displayed.
  2. Notice that an RSS icon appears with the search results. This enables you to watch the search in your favourite RSS reader. (If you take some time to look at the advanced search features, you will find that you can generate RSS feeds of all sorts of information in the index.)
