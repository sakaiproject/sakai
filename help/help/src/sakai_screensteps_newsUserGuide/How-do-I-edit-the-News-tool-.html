How do I edit the News tool?

Go to News.

Select the News item from the Tool Menu of your site.

(If a News item is not already active in the site, you will need to to Site Info > Manage Tools to add it to your site first.)

Click Edit.

Enter or edit the News item information and save.

  1. Enter/edit a tool title.
  2. Enter/edit the maximum number of items to display from this feed.
  3. Enter/edit the URL of your RSS feed.
  4. Click Save Configuration to save your changes.

Your changes will display.
