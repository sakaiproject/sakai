How do I print a report?

Go to Statistics.

Select the Statistics tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

View a report.

Select to view a report either by creating/running a report, or by clicking on one of the report links from the Overview page.

Click the Printable Version link.

Click Send to printer.

Your report will open in a new window for easier printing. Click on the Send to printer link in the top left corner to print your report.
