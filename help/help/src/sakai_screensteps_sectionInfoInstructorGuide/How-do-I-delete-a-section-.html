How do I delete a section?

Go to Section Info.

Select the Section Info tool from the Tool Menu in your site.

Select the section(s) to be deleted.

Zoom

In the Remove column, check the box(es) for the section(s) you would like to delete.

Click Remove Sections.

Confirm removal.

Zoom

You will be prompted to confirm the deletion of the selected sections. If you want to proceed, click Remove.
