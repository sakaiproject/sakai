How do I watch or subscribe to a wiki?

Go to Wiki.

Select the Wiki tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click Watch.

Zoom

Choose your notification preference.

Zoom

You may choose from the following notification options:

  • Each time a page is changed in this wiki (or wiki sub-section), send a separate email
  • Send me one email per day summarizing all changes in this wiki (or wiki sub-section)
  • Do not send me emails when pages change in this wiki (or wiki sub-section)
  • (For wiki sub-sections only) Just do the same as for the main wiki notifications

Select the radio button for your desired notification level, and then click Save.
