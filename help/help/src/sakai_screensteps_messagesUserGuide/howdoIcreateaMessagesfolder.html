How do I create a messages folder?

Add new folders with unique titles to store or reorganize your messages. Note that the Received, Sent, Deleted, Draft, and Scheduled folders will be provided by default.

Go to Messages.

Select the Messages tool from the Tool Menu in your site.

Go to New Folder.

Click New Folder.

Enter a folder title.

Enter a title into the Folder Title field.

Click Add.

View the new folder in your list of message folders.

The new folder will appear at the bottom of your list of message folders.

Note: See How do I move a message? for information about moving messages into the new folder.
