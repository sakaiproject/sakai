How do I view my messages?

Go to Messages.

Select the Messages tool from the Tool Menu in your site.

Go to Received folder.

Select the Received folder to view a list of your received messages.

Open the message.

New messages will appear in bold text. Click the subject of a message in the Subject column to view the message's contents.

Note: messages that are accompanied by a paperclip icon (Has attachment(s)) contain a file that was attached by the sender.

View the message.

The contents of the message will appear. The following options will also be available to you:

  1. Reply: Click Reply to reply to the original sender with a message of your own.
  2. Reply to all: Click Reply to all to reply to the original sender and any other course members included on the original message.
  3. Forward: Click Forward to forward the message to another member of the course.
  4. Move to folder: Click Move to folder to move the message from one folder to another.
  5. Delete: Click Delete to delete the message.
  6. If an attached file is available with the message, click the file's icon to download and view it.
