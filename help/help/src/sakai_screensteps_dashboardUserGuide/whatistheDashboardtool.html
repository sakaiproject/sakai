What is the Dashboard tool?

The Dashboard tool summarizes information about activity and events in your sites. You may see the Dashboard tool either in the Home area when you log in to the system, or within an individual course or project site. When viewed in Home, the Dashboard shows information from all of your current sites. When viewed within a site, the Dashboard displays information specific to that individual site.

To access this tool, select Dashboard from the Tool Menu in your site.
