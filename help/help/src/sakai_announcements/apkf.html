To create an announcement, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Announcements.

  2. Click New... .

  3. In the form that appears, type the subject and text of your announcement.

    Note: Fields marked with a red dot are required.

  4. If you want to add an attachment to your announcement, follow these steps:

    1. Click the Add Attachments button.
    2. You can add any number of attachments, and they can be any combination of local files, websites, and resources.

      • To attach a local file from your computer, click the Local File... button and then either enter a filename in the box provided or click Browse. Once you find the file, select it and click Open, then click Attach, and finally click Save to attach the item(s) to your announcement.

        Note: When uploading or attaching a file, you must include the file's extension (e.g., .gif, .doc, .html, or .jpg). If you do not, anyone who attempts to display or download the file will get an error message.

      • To attach a URL, click the Website URL... button and then type the URL in the box provided. Click Attach, and then click Save.
      • To attach an item from the "Resources" section of your website, click the From Resources... button. Check the box(es) next to the resource(s) you want to attach, then click Continue, and then click Save.

  5. You can choose to have your announcement emailed to participants in addition to being posted. From the "Email Notification:" field, select the appropriate option:

    • If you do not want the announcement emailed, choose None - No notification (the default setting).
    • If you want to send it only to those participants who have chosen to receive low-priority mail notifications, choose Low - Only participants who have opted in.
    • If you want all participants to receive it, choose High - All participants.

      Note: Participants have the option to choose whether they want to receive all announcements or only high priority announcements.

  6. You can either post the announcement, preview it, or save it as a draft. Drafts are visible to anyone with "maintain" access to the site.

    • To save your announcement as a draft, click Save Draft.
    • To see how your announcement will look, click Preview.
    • To post your announcement, click Post.
