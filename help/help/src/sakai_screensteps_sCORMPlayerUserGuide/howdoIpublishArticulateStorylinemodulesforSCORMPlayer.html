How do I publish Articulate Storyline modules for SCORM Player?

Publish your Articulate Storyline Module. This will open a dialogue box. Under the LMS tab of the dialogue box, click the Reporting and Tracking button.

Select SCORM 2004 from the "LMS" dropdown menu. Then select 3rd Edition from the "Edition" dropdown menu.

Note: The SCORM Player only supports SCORM 2004, 3rd edition.

Click OK and then click Publish.

Once you have your folder saved, you will have to compress it into a zip file.
