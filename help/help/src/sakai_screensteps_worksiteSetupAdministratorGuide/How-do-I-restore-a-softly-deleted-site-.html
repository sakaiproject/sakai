How do I restore a softly deleted site?

Go to Worksite Setup.

Select the Worksite Setup tool from the Tool Menu.

From the View drop-down menu, select Softly Deleted Sites.

Zoom

All sites marked for deletion will be displayed.

Zoom

Click the Hard Delete button located at the top or the bottom of the screen.

Note: If you do not want to permanently delete the site and all of its files and data, you may choose the Delete button instead to perform a soft delete.

Select the site you want to restore, then click Restore.
