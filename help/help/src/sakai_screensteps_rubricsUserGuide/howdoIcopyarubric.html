How do I copy a rubric?

Tip: Copying a rubric and editing the copy may be faster than creating a new rubric.

Go to the Rubrics tool.

Select the Rubrics tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click Copy icon.

Click the Copy icon (Copy Rubric) associated with a rubric that you wish to copy.

Enter a new Rubric Title.

  1. Update the title.
  2. Click Save.

Update the rubric's information and scoring. (Optional)

See How do I edit a rubric? for information about fully updating the copied rubric.
