How do I view my rubric feedback as a student?

To view feedback that was given using a rubric, visit the tool where the rubric was used. For instance, to view feedback about an assignment that was graded using a rubric, go to the Assignments tool. To view feedback about an assessment that used a rubric, go to the Tests & Quizzes tool.

Viewing rubric feedback in the Assignments tool

Go to the Assignments tool.

Click on a returned assignment.

View the graded rubric.

Move down the page until you locate the Grading Rubric section. The selections in each criterion are highlighted for you, and the awarded points for each criterion displayed to you to the right of each criterion row.

The points total are displayed to you in the Total area below the rubric.

Viewing rubric feedback in the Gradebook tool

Go to the Gradebook tool.

Click on the item's rubric icon.

Click the rubric icon (Preview Rubric) associated with the grade that you'd like to check.

View the graded rubric.

The selections in each criterion are highlighted for you, and the awarded points for each criterion displayed to you to the right of each criterion row.

The points total are displayed to you in the Total area below the rubric.

Viewing rubric feedback in the Tests & Quizzes tool

Go to the Tests & Quizzes tool.

Click the Feedback link for the assessment.

View the graded rubric.

The selections in each criterion are highlighted for you, and the awarded points for each criterion displayed to you to the right of each criterion row.

The points total are displayed to you in the Total area below the rubric.
