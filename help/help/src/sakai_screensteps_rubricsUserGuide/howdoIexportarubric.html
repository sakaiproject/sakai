How do I export a rubric?

Export a rubric as a PDF document to store for your records or distribute for offline grading.

Go to the Rubrics tool.

Select the Rubrics tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click the Export icon.

Click the PDF icon (Export) associated with a rubric.

Verify the downloaded file.

The rubric will download to your device as a PDF file. Open the PDF file using an applicable program to view the downloaded rubric.
