What is the admin Announcements tool or Message of the Day (MOTD)?

In the Overview area of Home, you will see announcements from your system administrator under "Message of the Day" (MOTD). The MOTD is typically used for system-wide announcements to inform users about upcoming events or important information.  

System administrators access the MOTD functions using the Announcements tool in the Administration Workspace.
