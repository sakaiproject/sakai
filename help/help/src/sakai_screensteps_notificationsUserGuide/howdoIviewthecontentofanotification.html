How do I view the content of a notification?

If you have any new notifications, you can view them by clicking on the item to view more information. Notifications are grouped by tool and date.

Click on the notification bell icon in the top navigation to view a list of all current alerts.

Click the drop down arrow next to the tool notification(s) you want to view.

Click on the arrow for an individual alert item to go directly to that item within the site.

The selected item will display.
