How do I print the roster?

Note: Depending on your role and permissions, and the individual privacy settings on user profiles, some of the features below may not be available.

Go to Roster.

Select the Roster tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click Print.

The PDF file of the list of users enrolled in the site will be display for printing.

Note: The currently selected view (cards, photo grid, or list) will be the format of the roster PDF.
