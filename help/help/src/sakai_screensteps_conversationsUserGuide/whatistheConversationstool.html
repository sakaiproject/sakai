What is the Conversations tool?

The Conversations tool enables instructors and students to ask, answer, and reply to questions. Instructors can organize Conversation topics by creating and applying tags, and can share them with specific groups. Students can ask questions, add tags created by the instructor, and respond to questions and answers with both text and emojis. Additionally, important topics can be pinned for easy access. Tags can be used to sort topics and topics can also be searched.

To access this tool, select Conversations from the Tool Menu of your site.
