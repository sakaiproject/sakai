How may I customize my presence in Conversations?

In Conversations, users will have a limited profile that displays alongside posts they create. This includes their Sakai-provided display name and a profile image as it appears in Sakai’s Profile tool. You can adjust certain information within the Profile tool, for more information please see Profile tool documentation.

Users with the instructor permission enabled in Sakai will have the role of Instructor displayed next to their name when they answer questions. This allows participants to easily distinguish the Instructor’s Answer, which is also highlighted with a light green background
