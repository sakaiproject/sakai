Reviewing or modifying your profile

To review or modify your profile:

  1. In the menubar of My Workspace, click Profile.

    You will see all the information entered for your profile. However, in this view, you will not see any fields for which no information has been entered. Those fields are visible, even if there is no information for them, in the profile that others see when they click your name in the Roster tool of a site of which you are a member.

  2. To see all the profile fields, or to modify your profile, click Edit my Profile.

  3. Add, change, or hide information as you wish. If you want to add a picture to your profile, see Adding a picture to your profile.

  4. When you're finished with your changes, click Save.
