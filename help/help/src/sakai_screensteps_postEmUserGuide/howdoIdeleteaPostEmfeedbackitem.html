How do I delete a PostEm feedback item?

Go to PostEm.

Select the PostEm tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Note: The PostEm tool is sometimes named Feedback.

Find the feedback item and click the delete link.

For the feedback item you would like to delete and click the delete link.

Verify and click the Delete button.

Verify that this is the correct title of the feedback item you want to delete and click the Delete button.
