How do I comment/reply to a post in Commons?

Go to the Commons tool.

Select the Commons tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Enter a comment/reply.

Locate a post to which you would like to reply.

  1. Click Reply.
  2. Enter a reply in the text editor.
  3. Click Post Comment.

Verify your comment/reply.

After you have entered your comment, it will display to you and other site participants beneath the post that you commented on.
