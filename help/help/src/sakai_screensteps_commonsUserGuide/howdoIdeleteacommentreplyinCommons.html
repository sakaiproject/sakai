How do I delete a comment/reply in Commons?

You must possess delete permissions in the Commons tool in order to delete a comment/reply. Deleting a comment/reply is a permanent action.

Go to the Commons tool.

Select the Commons tool from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click Delete.

Locate the comment/reply that you would like to delete and click its associated Delete option.

Confirm deletion.

Click OK to confirm permanent deletion of the comment/reply.
