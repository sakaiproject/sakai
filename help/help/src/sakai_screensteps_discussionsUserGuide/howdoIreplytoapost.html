How do I reply to a post?

Go to Discussions.

Select Discussions from the Tool Menu of your site.

Click on a Topic.

Click on a conversation.

Click reply.

  1. Click Reply to Initial Message to reply to the first message in the conversation.
  2. Click Reply to reply to the message you are currently viewing. This is the option to use if you wish to reply to someone else's reply.

Compose your message.

  1. Enter a the title of your response in the Reply Title field.
  2. Optionally click Insert original textÂ to include the text of the original post in your reply.
  3. Enter content in the Message field using the Rich Text Editor.
  4. Optionally click the Add attachments button to browse for and select a file from your device.
  5. Click Post.
