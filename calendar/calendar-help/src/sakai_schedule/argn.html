Adding an attachment to a schedule item

Note: To complete the procedures in this document, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and roles: Overview.

To add an attachment to an item in Schedule:

  1. In the menubar, click Schedule.

  2. To add a new item, see Creating/adding, editing, or deleting Schedule items. For an existing item, click the item's name, and then click Edit.

  3. Under "Attachments", you can attach a file from your local computer or from Resources, or specify the URL for a file on the web. For instructions, see Options for adding attachments.

  4. Make any updates to your schedule item, and then click Save Event.
