How do I create a Syllabus?

  1. Click the title of your project site or course site to enter it.

  2. If you do not see the Syllabus tool in the menu bar, you must add it to your site.

  3. From the menu bar, click Syllabus.

  4. Click Create/Edit.

  5. To find out how to display a webpage as your syllabus, click the link "Creating syllabus as webpage"
