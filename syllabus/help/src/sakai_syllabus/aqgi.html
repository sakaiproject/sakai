How do I add, edit, or delete a syllabus item?

Note:The Syllabus tool is available from the menubar in your project or course site. If you do not see the Syllabus tool in your site's menu bar, you must add it to your site.

Adding a syllabus item

  1. Click New.

  2. Enter a title for your syllabus item, and then enter your content. You can copy and paste text from another file, such as HTML or a Microsoft Word document.

  3. Choose whether to have the item viewable only by members of the site or by anyone (Public View).

  4. Choose whether you would like to have members of the site notified automatically via email when the resource is posted.

  5. When you have finished adding your syllabus item, click Post to post it to your site, Preview to view it, Save Draft to save it as a draft, or Cancel to cancel.

  6. Click Update.

Editing a syllabus item

To edit a syllabus item, click the name of the item you wish to edit, and then follow steps 2-6 above.

Deleting a syllabus item

To delete a syllabus item, to the right of the item you wish to remove, check the box under "Remove". Click Update.
