Grading an assignment or reviewing student status

Note: To complete the procedures in this document, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and roles: Overview.

Grading an assignment

To grade an assignment, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click Assignments. You will see a list of assignments.

  2. Under the name of the assignment you want to grade, click Grade. You will see the assignment along with student submissions.

    Note: To enter the same grade for all students without submissions, enter the grade next to "Assign this grade to all participants without submissions:" and click Apply.

  3. To view a student's submitted assignment and start grading, click the student's name.

    Note: To type comments into student text, enclose your comments in double curly brackets, for example:

    {{Cite your sources, please.}}

    Your comments will appear in red. You can enter additional summary comments into the box below the student text (no special formatting required).

  4. If you would like to add to the assignment an attachment to give to the student, click Add Attachments. For instructions, see Options for adding attachments.

  5. Next to "Grade", select the grade for the student's assignment.

  6. If you wish to allow the student to resubmit the assignment, next to "Number of resubmissions allowed", select the number of submissions you would like to allow.

    Note: If you choose this option, you will also need to select a date and time next to "Accept Until".

  7. When grading is complete, click Return to student to make the student's assignment grade immediately available, or click Save Draft to return to the submitted assignments list to continue grading. You may also click Preview to see how the returned assignment will look to the student. Or, click Cancel to return to the assignment list.

    Note: You can return assignments to students with or without grades.

  8. When you have finished grading student assignments and are ready to send grades to the Gradebook, when viewing the list of submitted assignments, click Release Grades.

Reviewing student status for all assignments

You can view a list of your students and see each one's status on each of the assignments. You can see whether the student has submitted the assignment, the assignment status, and the grade, if one has been given.

  1. In the menubar, click the Assignments button.

  2. From the "View" drop-down list, select Assignment List by Student.

  3. To view the status of a student's assignment submissions, click the black arrow next to the student's name. To hide the student's information, click the arrow again.
