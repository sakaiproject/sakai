Searching Messages

To search for text in a message:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Messages.

  2. Click the folder you wish to search.

  3. Next to "Search for text:", enter your search terms in the text box, and then click Search. The standard search will look for your terms in the subject line of the messages in the selected folder. Searches are case sensitive.

  4. To modify the search parameters, click Advanced Search and check the appropriate box(es). Available parameters are:

    • Subject: The subject line of the message
    • Authored By: Who sent the message
    • Body: The body of the message
    • Label: The label associated with the message
    • Date Range: The date range in which the message was sent

    Search parameters in advanced searches will remain checked for subsequent searches until you clear them. To clear a search, click Clear Search.

  5. To end an advanced search, click Normal Search.
