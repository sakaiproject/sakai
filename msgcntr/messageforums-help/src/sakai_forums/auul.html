Deleting a forum message

Note: To complete the procedures in this document, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and roles: Overview.

To delete a message in Forums:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Forums.

  2. Click the title of the desired topic.

  3. Find and click the message you want to delete, and then click Delete. Alternatively, you can click the title of the thread or Display Entire Message, and then, next to the message, click Other Actions, and select Delete.

  4. On the confirmation page, click Delete to delete the message.

Note: You can also delete the forum or topic entirely, which will remove any associated messages. For instructions, see Revising or deleting a forum or topic.
