Emailing forum message authors

In Forums, certain roles can directly email the author of a message in a forum. To do so:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Forums.

  2. Click the title of the forum, and then the title of the relevant topic.

  3. Click the title of a conversation to see all messages in the conversation. Each message will have an Email link under it; clicking the link will open your email client, allowing you to send mail to the author of that message.
