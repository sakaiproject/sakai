Grading messages in Forums

Note: Before following these directions you must first create an item in the Gradebook; for instructions, see Adding, editing, or deleting a Gradebook item.

To grade a message in Forums:

  1. In your site's menubar, click Forums.

  2. Click the title of the desired topic.

  3. Find and click the individual message to grade, and then click Grade. Alternatively, you can click the title of the thread or Display Entire Message, and then, next to an individual message, click the Other Actions menu, and select Grade.

  4. Enter a point value for a grade, and then use the drop-down list to select the item for which you're posting the grade. (It will automatically select a gradebook item if you selected one while creating the topic.)

  5. Next to "Comments", compose a message in the text box.

  6. When you're finished, click Submit Grade. To cancel, click Cancel.
