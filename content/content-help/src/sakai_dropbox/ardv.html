Moving Drop Box items to another folder

To copy item(s) from one Drop Box folder to another:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Drop Box.

  2. Click the black triangles to the left of "Title" to expand all the folders in the Drop Box, or click the folder icon to the left of the name of the folder you wish to expand. (If a folder contains items not currently displayed, a plus sign will appear on the folder icon.)

  3. Mark the checkbox next to the item(s) you want to copy, and click Copy.

  4. Click the "Paste copied items" icon  
      next to the folder in which you want to paste the item(s), or mouse over Actions and choose Paste copied items.

  5. If you wish to delete the original item, mark the checkbox next to the item in its original location and click Remove.

Instructors will see a yellow star highlighting Drop Box folders that have recently been updated.
