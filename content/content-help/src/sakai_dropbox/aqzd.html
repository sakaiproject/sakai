Viewing Drop Boxes and their contents

To view the Drop Boxes available for a site, in the menubar, click Drop Box. You will see a list of Drop Boxes and/or folders containing them.

To view the contents of a Drop Box, click the black triangles to the left of "Title", or click the folder icon to the left of the name of the folder or Drop Box you wish to expand. (If a folder contains items not currently being displayed, a plus sign will appear on the folder icon.) Instructors will see an orange star highlighting Drop Box folders that have recently been updated.

To open a Drop Box or folder, click its name.
