Copying an item in Resources

Note: To complete the procedures in this document, you must be assigned a role having the necessary permissions. To determine your role, follow the directions in Participant roles. For a basic understanding of roles and permissions, see Permissions and Roles: Overview.

To copy a resource, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click Resources.

  2. Find the resource you want to copy, and mark the checkbox to its left.

  3. Click Copy Checked. The resource will now be highlighted.

  4. Navigate to the location where you with to copy the file, and then click Paste Copied Items. If you do not see the folder to which you want to copy the resource, click the arrow next to "Show other sites"; this will display the folders to which you have access.

    Note: To cancel the copy action, click the reset button (i.e., the circular arrow icon to the left of "Resources".
