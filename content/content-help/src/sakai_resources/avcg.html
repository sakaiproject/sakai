Deleting a Resources item

To delete an item from Resources:

  1. To delete one item at a time, from the Actions menu, choose Remove. To delete several items at a time, mark their corresponding checkboxes, and then, above the list of items, click Remove.

  2. To confirm that you want to delete the item(s) listed, click Remove. Or, to cancel, click Cancel.

Note: You can delete a folder containing items. If you wish to keep the files stored in the folder, first move or copy its contents into another folder before deleting the folder.
