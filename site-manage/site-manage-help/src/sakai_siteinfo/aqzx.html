Viewing site information

In the menubar of a course site or project site, you can click Site Info to view information about the site. If your role has restricted permissions for the site, you will see only the information published by the site's owner. However, if your role includes a broad level of permissions, you will see a participant list as well as buttons that allow you to edit that list and other site information. For editing information, see Editing information about your site with Site Info.
